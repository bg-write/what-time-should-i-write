"What Time Should I Write?" was created by Brady Gerber, a morning person, and was mostly built with Python, Django, and Amazon AWS.

Routines are cited on each writer's individual page. All photos are taken from Wikipedia.

I aim to be as accurate as possible, only sharing the routines that each writer has talked about on record. Sometimes, writers have multiple routines. Sometimes, they change and fine-tune how they work. Some routines are super detailed. Others are vague, and act more like guidelines. Some of my favorite writers never discussed their routines. I tried my best to present each writer's approach towards creativity to stay as true as possible to how they worked.

I'm adding new writers and updating this database when I can. Any writers you want me to add? Spot any errors? Connect and let me know!
