"What Time Should I Write?" was created by Brady Gerber, a morning person.

All sources of writer routines are cited on their pages.

I aim to be as accurate as possible, only sharing routines that writers have talked about on record. Sometimes writers have multiple routines. Sometimes they change their routines over the years. Some are super detailed. Others are vague and are more guidelines than routines. I tried my best to present each writer approach to creativity in an informative way while staying as true to how their worked.

I'm constantly adding new writers and updating this database when I can.

Are there any writers who you want me to add to this database? Connect and let me know!
